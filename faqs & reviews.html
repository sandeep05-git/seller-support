Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: How do I create a product listing?
To create a product listing, log in to your seller dashboard, click on "Add Product," fill in the details like title, description, price, and images, then submit for approval.
Q2: How do I manage returns?
Returns can be managed from the "Orders" section. Select the returned order and follow the instructions to approve or reject the return request.
Q3: How long does it take to get paid?
Payments are usually processed within 7-10 business days after the order is completed and confirmed.

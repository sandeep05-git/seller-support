Top 5 Challenges Online Sellers Face in India (And How to Overcome Them)

With the growth of platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, and others, becoming an online seller in India has never been easier — but success isn't always smooth. Many sellers face serious challenges that can slow down or even stop their business.

1. Listing Errors

Wrong categories, GTIN issues, image rejections, and title problems are common — especially for new sellers. These errors can hide your product from search results.

2. Fake Returns & Buyer Fraud

Many sellers receive returned products that are used, damaged, or completely different. This leads to loss and affects seller ratings.

3. Account Suspension

Even small policy violations can result in account deactivation. Reinstating your account often needs a strong Plan of Action (POA).

4. Low Visibility & Sales

Without keyword optimization, good product images, and fast delivery, your products might not rank well or convert into sales.

5. Platform Support Delays

Sometimes, it’s hard to get quick answers from Amazon or Flipkart support teams — especially if you're a small seller.

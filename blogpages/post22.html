What is Seller Support?

Seller support is a team or service that helps you with your account, listings, payments, returns, policy issues, and more. They guide you step-by-step to fix problems and improve your performance.

Common Issues Seller Support Can Solve

  • Listing errors or product visibility problems
  • Account suspension or verification delays
  • Fake or fraudulent buyers
  • Negative reviews or order defects
  • Payment or invoice-related concerns

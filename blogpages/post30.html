How to Protect Yourself from Fake Buyers on Amazon, Flipkart & Meesho

Fake buyers and return fraud are growing concerns for online sellers in India. Many sellers have faced issues like receiving old or broken products in return, or getting scammed by customers misusing return/refund policies.

Whether you're selling on Amazon, Flipkart, or Meesho, protecting yourself from fake buyers is critical for maintaining profit and account health.

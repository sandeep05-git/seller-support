🛒 eCommerce News Update – May 2025

Published on May 16, 2025

🔁 Return Policy Tightened for High-Return Categories

Leading marketplaces have revised their return policies. Categories like electronics and fashion are now under tighter return scrutiny. Sellers are advised to improve product descriptions and images to reduce return rates.

💸 Seller Fee Adjustments Announced

From May 20th, commission fees in select categories will increase by 1.5%. This change affects low-margin categories such as home essentials and health products. Review your pricing to maintain profitability.

🛠️ New Tools for Inventory and Analytics

Major platforms are rolling out dashboard upgrades. You’ll now find improved analytics for tracking returns, inventory status, and keyword performance. Use this data to make smarter decisions and boost your rankings.

🧾 Important Reminder: GST Filings for Sellers

Small business sellers are reminded to file their GST by May 20 to avoid penalties. Government portals are expected to experience traffic—file early to avoid delays.

What This Means for New Sellers

  • Double-check your return policies and item listings.
  • Monitor fee changes and adjust product prices accordingly.
  • Start using the new analytics dashboard to track your performance.
  • File GST on time if registered as a seller with a GSTIN.

📢 Stay informed. Check back regularly for news, tips, and updates that matter to your selling journey!

How to Get Your Amazon Seller Account Unsuspended in 2025

If your Amazon seller account has been suspended, you're not alone. Thousands of sellers in India face suspensions every year due to order defect rates, late shipments, intellectual property complaints, and more.

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you recover your account and avoid future problems — shared by EcoQuickSupp, India's trusted online seller support platform.

🔍 Step 1: Understand Why You Were Suspended

Amazon sends you a performance notification that explains the reason for the suspension. Read it carefully — it could be due to:

  • High order defect rate (ODR)
  • Late shipment rate or cancellation issues
  • Inauthentic or counterfeit complaints
  • Policy violations

📝 Step 2: Write a Strong Plan of Action (POA)

Your POA should clearly include three things:

  • Root Cause: Explain what led to the issue.
  • Immediate Action Taken: What did you do to fix it?
  • Preventive Measures: How will you avoid it in the future?

Be honest, clear, and professional. Avoid blaming Amazon or the customer.

📤 Step 3: Submit the Appeal

Go to your Amazon Seller Central → Performance → Account Health, and click on “Reactivate Your Account” or “Appeal Decision.” Paste your POA and attach any required documents (like invoices or screenshots).

⏳ Step 4: Wait for Amazon’s Reply

Amazon may take 24–72 hours to respond. If your POA is rejected, revise it based on their comments and resubmit. You may get 2–3 chances to appeal.
